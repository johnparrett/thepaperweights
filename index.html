The Paperweights

About the Band

The Paperweights are a two-piece alternative rock band that joined the Columbus music scene in early 2017.

Having played together for nearly a decade, singer/guitar Taylor Day and drummer John Parrett play a mix of original and cover music. Catch them at various locations in and around Central Ohio!
