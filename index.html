The Paperweights

About the Band

The Paperweights, a duo that started making music in 2017, quickly became part of the Central Ohio music scene. They play a mix of popular covers and their original songs.

They've performed at a variety of venues, from holiday festivals and cozy brewery taprooms to corporate events, concert halls, street corners, bars, and restaurants. The band is always ready to play at different locations.

You can see The Paperweights performing at various places in and around Central Ohio. Don't miss the chance to enjoy their music live!
